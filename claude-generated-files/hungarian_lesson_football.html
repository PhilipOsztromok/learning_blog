⚽ Football — Foci

Six short conversations about football — from watching a match to celebrating a goal. Natural, everyday Hungarian your family will recognise.

Pronunciation quick-guide: cs = "ch" (church) sz = "s" (sun) zs = "zh" (measure) gy = soft "dj" ny = "ny" (canyon) j / ly = "y" (yes) s = "sh" (shop)

① Watching Together — Együtt nézni

A: Nézel ma focit? Are you watching football today?
B: Igen! Magyarország játszik este. Yes! Hungary is playing tonight.
A: Hol nézzük a meccset? Where shall we watch the match?
B: Nálam. Hozz sört! At mine. Bring beer!

② The Score — Az Eredmény

A: Mi az eredmény? What's the score?
B: Kettő-egy! Mi vezetünk! Two-one! We're leading!
A: Remek! Hajrá, Magyarország! Great! Come on, Hungary!

③ Buying Tickets — Jegyvétel

A: Van még jegy a meccsre? Are there still tickets for the match?
B: Igen, van. Hány darabot kér? Yes, there are. How many do you want?
A: Kettőt kérek, köszönöm. Two please, thank you.

④ Goal! — Gól!

A: GÓÓÓL! Láttad? lát-tad = did you see it GOAAAL! Did you see it?
B: Igen! Gyönyörű gól volt! gyönyörű = "dyö-nyö-rű" = beautiful Yes! It was a beautiful goal!
A: Ez a legjobb meccs! This is the best match!

⑤ After the Match — A Meccs Után

A: Milyen volt a meccs? How was the match?
B: Sajnos vesztettünk. Kettő-nulla. Unfortunately we lost. Two-nil.
A: Legközelebb jobb lesz! It'll be better next time!

⑥ Favourite Team — Kedvenc Csapat

A: Mi a kedvenc csapatod? What is your favourite team?
B: A Ferencváros. És neked? Ferencváros. And yours?
A: Én az Újpestnek szurkolok. I support Újpest.
B: Akkor riválisok vagyunk! Then we are rivals!

Grammar Tip — a vs. az (The Definite Article): Hungarian uses a before words starting with a consonant and az before words starting with a vowel — similar to "a" and "an" in English. So it's a csapat (the team) but az edző (the coach) and az eredmény (the score). You'll notice this pattern everywhere, and getting it right will instantly impress your Hungarian family! As a bonus cultural note: football (foci) is serious business in Hungary — Ferencváros vs. Újpest is one of Europe's oldest club rivalries, so knowing your csapatok (teams) will go down very well.
